Privacy Policy

WiFi Analyze  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Short version: WiFi Analyze does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything the app reads stays on your device.

What the app does

WiFi Analyze reads your device's WiFi state to display signal strength, nearby networks, channel congestion, and connection details. It can save room-by-room signal readings and speed/latency test results to help you optimize your home network.

Data collected

WiFi Analyze collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No data is sent to any server operated by the developer.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only, using Android's Room database and DataStore:

  • Room-by-room signal readings you manually save (room name, signal strength, timestamp)
  • Speed test results (download/upload Mbps, timestamp, network name)
  • Latency test results (ping times, timestamp, network name)
  • App preferences (display mode, alert threshold, dark mode setting)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time via Settings → Clear All Saved Rooms, or by uninstalling the app.

Permissions used

  • Approximate Location — Required by Android to scan for nearby WiFi networks and read network names (SSIDs). The app does not use your location for any other purpose and does not store GPS coordinates.
  • Nearby Devices / WiFi State — Used to read your current WiFi connection details (signal strength, channel, band, IP address).
  • Notifications — Optional. Used only to send signal-weakness alerts you configure in Settings. No notifications are sent without your explicit opt-in.
  • Internet — Used only when you manually run a speed test (connects to Cloudflare's speed test servers). Not used at any other time.

Third-party services

The only external network request the app makes is the optional speed test, which contacts speed.cloudflare.com. Cloudflare's own privacy policy applies to that request.

The "Buy Me a Pizza" button opens buymeacoffee.com in your browser. That site's own privacy policy applies if you choose to visit it.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

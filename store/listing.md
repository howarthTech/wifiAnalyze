## Full Description

**Know your WiFi. Fix your home network.**

WiFi Analyze gives you real-time intelligence about your wireless network — from simple signal ratings to advanced channel analysis, speed testing, and room-by-room coverage mapping.

**Simple Mode — Perfect for everyday users**
• See your signal strength in plain English: Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor, or No Signal
• Walk room-to-room and log readings to find dead spots
• Get router placement recommendations based on where signal is strongest and weakest
• Check IoT device readiness (smart home devices need a solid 2.4 GHz signal)
• See competing networks and channel congestion at a glance

**Advanced Mode — For network enthusiasts**
• Live signal history chart with a stability score (Stable → Unstable)
• Download and upload speed test via Cloudflare — no account needed
• Ping latency to your router gateway and internet (8.8.8.8)
• Channel usage bar chart showing which channels are congested
• Channel spectrum overlap view — see exactly which networks are competing
• Mesh/multi-AP detection: see all nodes of your network with signal per node
• Connection details: IP, gateway, DNS, TX/RX link speed
• Network Score graded A–F, combining signal, stability, speed, and latency
• Export scan data as JSON to share with your ISP or router manufacturer

**History & Trends**
• Signal strength recorded every 5 minutes while the app is open
• Speed test and latency test results saved over time
• View 24-hour or 7-day history charts for signal, speed, and latency

**Home Screen Widget**
• At-a-glance signal quality widget — tap it to open the app
• Updates automatically in the background every 15 minutes

**Smart Notifications**
• Optional alerts when your signal drops below a threshold you set
• "All clear" notification when signal recovers
• Channel congestion alert (max once per day) when your channel is overcrowded

**Privacy**
• All data stays on your device
• No account, no sign-up, no data collection
• Nothing is uploaded or shared
